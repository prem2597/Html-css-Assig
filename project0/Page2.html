Achievements


Jenesys 2017

Japan-East Asia Network of Exchange for Students and Youths is a project advanced by the Japanese government from the standpoint of providing a sound foundation for strong solidarity within Asia through large-scale youth exchange. I was selected as one of the candidate among 26 pupil from India, and visited Japan in the month March 2018 on behalf of representing India. JENESYS is a Science, Technology and Culture exchange program between India and Japan. I was one of the candidate among two pupil who were selected from Andhra Pradesh and Telangana and visited Japan and represented behalf of India. JICE is Japan International Cooperation Center who has funded this trip on behalf of Japan Government. I was appointed as an Honorary Ambassador of Tourism of Kuji City in recognition of successful completion of the JENESYS 2017, held in the City of Kuji,Iwate Prefecture by the Mayor of Kuji city Joji ENDO on March 25,2018. We have also had a workshop presentation in the presence of Sachiko Yamano the President of Japan International Cooperation Center and received a certificate for the completion of JENESYS 2017.
